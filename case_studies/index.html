Vermont Hackathon

The Agilion Apps team took first place in the 2012 Vermont Hackathon with a web-based market analysis tool called "The Vermont Business Landscape", designed to help potential business owners understand the health of various business sectors in Vermont.

Our team believes in the value of small businesses to Vermontâ€™s economy, and in doing research, we discovered that the state has a need for aggregating business data from disparate data sources. The Burlington Community & Economic Development Office (CEDO) receives frequent inquiries from prospective new business owners as to the health of various sectors throughout Vermont. By finding real people who had a problem, we were able to validate our idea and build confidence going into the competition.

In order to develop a working prototype in 24 hours, we spent time planning up front, and focused on building a minimum viable product that would demonstrate the value of modernizing Vermont's business data.
