Events

Burlington Ruby Conference

Agilion co-organizes the Burlington Ruby Conference in Burlington, VT. This conference is a 2-day, single track event with a focus on Ruby. Unlike other conferences, we strive to make the experience as relaxing and friendly as possible. Visit burlingtonruby.com for more information about this year's conference.

User Groups

Burlington Web App Group

The Burlington Web App Group (BTVWAG) was founded in 2010 by three current Agilioneers. Our vision for the group was a place for technology enthusiasts focused on web development to come together and share ideas. We have grown to over 200 members and have hosted numerous meet ups including talks on behavior driven development, software craftsmanship, and various JavaScript topics. Interested technologists can also subscribe to our email newsletter containing upcoming events across many Vermont software related user groups.

Burlington Ruby Group

The Burlington Ruby Group is a Ruby users group located in Burlington, VT, co-organized by Agilion Apps and Gristmill. Founded in 2012 by members of BTVWAG, the group has become a proponent for Ruby and other related open source technologies in Vermont. We meet in Burlington multiple times a year; the event schedule can be found on our group's website.

Burlington JS

Burlington JS is a JavaScript users group located in Burlington, VT and organized by Agilion Apps and other members of the Burlington community. The event schedule can be found on our group's website.
